Launch results

Findings after implementation:

  1. The feature is currently launched and live on the platform for over 75000+ users, and actively used by other web platform designers to announce new features!
  2. Increased speed of the design team launching new feature, with the use of the template
  3. Increased visibility and discovery of new features released
  4. Positive feedback from users, especially regarding the ability to test out features right from the announcement

Takeaways

  1. I learned how important it was to stay broad and take versatility highly into account when making template design decisions! There are many unknowns for the use cases of a template, so my thinking needed to be adaptive and conceptual.
  2. I also learned about avoiding getting caught into the details, as working with content and visual designers emphasized my focus on designing for edge cases!
