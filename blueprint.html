Designing for social good on

UW Blueprint

A group of ~80+ UWaterloo students building technology for non-profits

CONTEXT

Since fall 2020, I’ve been volunteering my time to UW Blueprint, a club aiming to make technology accessible to community non-profits without the funding. Each term I’ve got to work on a project team with other students, and each project team partners with a specific non-profit to build a solution to help equip them with tools they need.

I’m super grateful to have been on blueprint for 2 terms (and counting!) and it has been so wonderful to be able to give back to the community and see the impact we can make working together. In fall 2020, I designed for the Region of Waterloo paramedics project team, in which we were building a mass casualty patient tracking application, to help paramedics better navigate through large mass casulty events. Last term in winter 2021, I designed for Raising the Roof project team, in which we were working on a storefront, team dashboard, and admin dashboard, for their annual toque campaign for homelessness.
